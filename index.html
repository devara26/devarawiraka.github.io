01 — About

Hi, I'm
Devara Wiraka

Graphic designer and content creator based in Jakarta. I've been doing this since 2020 — helping brands, organizations, and institutions translate their message into visuals that actually work.

From government ministries to student campaigns, I've handled it all — design, photography, short-form video, and everything in between. Most recently at the Ministry of Marine Affairs and Fisheries (KKP RI), where I produced creative content for national-scale communications.

I work best when given creative freedom — but I always keep the audience and the message in mind first.
